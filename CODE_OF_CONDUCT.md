# Code of Conduct

## Our Standards

Contributors are expected to keep discussion professional, specific, and
focused on improving the project. Good-faith critique is welcome. Harassment,
personal attacks, discriminatory language, doxxing, and sustained disruption
are not acceptable.

## Scope

This code of conduct applies to project spaces including issues, pull
requests, discussions, documentation, and any community venue explicitly
associated with `code-review-loop`.

## Enforcement

Report conduct concerns to `colinfarmer.gg1@gmail.com`. Reports will be
handled privately and proportionately. Maintainers may remove content, lock
threads, block users, or take other action needed to protect the project
community.

## Attribution

This policy is written for this project and follows the same general principles
as widely used open-source community codes of conduct.
